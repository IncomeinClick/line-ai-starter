# When to hand a conversation to a human

The brain already escalates anything about money, payments, receipts, refunds,
billing, or a customer's specific order. Use this file to add your own rules —
topics you'd always rather a human handle.

Example:

- Always hand off if the customer is upset or wants to cancel.
- Hand off any question about custom or bulk orders.
- Hand off anything you're not confident answering.
